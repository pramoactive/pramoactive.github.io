  • Overview
Our Technology Consulting team partners with you to understand your business processes for trade generation, systems processing, data communication, reconciliation and internal information flows. As one of the most experienced groups in the industry, we coordinate, develop and implement customized integration strategies for all technology-related services that we provide.

After assessing an organization's current systems and workflow patterns, our consultants determine your present and future needs and learn about your internal systems, operational processes, time constraints and potential integration challenges. We then make recommendations for updating and streamlining your current processes, offering a combination of customized best-of-breed services, as well as external resources. We work in conjunction with you and the operations team assigned to your account to make automation recommendations and assist in solution implementation.

Providing technical relationship management services and acting as a technical focal point to address all technology inquiries, our team can help you:

Achieve reduced operational risk by reducing manual processing
Improve the timeliness of information delivery
Enhance your access to data
Streamline integration between primary and downstream applications
Access more complete, accurate and timely information to make better-informed decisions

EITS’s Technology Consulting practice supports all phases of the systems project lifecycle to help you achieve success.
