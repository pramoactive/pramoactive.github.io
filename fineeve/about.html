Company Profile

Everest IT Services Pvt. Ltd. is a leading Software Products and Services company based out of Mumbai, India serving a growing customer base through our extensive partner network in Middle East & Africa. We integrate the power of visual computing with enterprise applications to deliver innovative, interactive and integrated solutions.

We provide solutions that go beyond just implementation of technology. Our approach envisions that business and technology go hand in hand. We focus on understanding our clients' vision, business objectives and strategy and then design unique tailor made solutions that add value to their organization.

We are pioneers in the use of the latest technologies for providing our customer best End-to-End solutions. We have team of dedicated professionals having the passion and desire to excel in the adverse conditions and satisfy the clients.

We are differentiated by our full-service portfolio that allows us to address diverse customer needs and deliver an integrated, one-stop solution.

Our customers work with us across service lines - using the depth and breadth of our offering to align IT strategy with evolving business needs. Our enterprise Solutions reflects both strategic intent and operational efficiency.

“WE WORK INLINE WITH OUR CUSTOMERS”
By adding industry experience and expertise, Everest Advanced delivers industry solutions that measurably benefit our customers in the Banking Sector, Financial Services, Telecommunications, Governments, Utilities, Manufacturing, Trading, Hospitality and other sectors.

Our product portfolio includes
Advanced Reception Management (ARM) – Comprehensive Q & Reception Management solution
Advanced Cheque Automation(ACA) – Cheque deposition and CTS solution
Advanced Bill Payment (ABP) – Bill payment kiosk specially designed and developed for self service
Digital Signage (ADS) – Tool to market and showcase digital signage
